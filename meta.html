META

My reason for making layout tool used by the home page was for use in displaying programs written in Silk, the language I am creating. Programs in Silk are graphs contained in a group, which is just a special node. Groups have children as nodes, and some of those children may be groups, but each node is the child of exactly one group. More info about Silk can be found here.

The home page is displayed as a program would be, with each box representing a group node. Each group also has each of its children as inputs to enforce order on the nodes, but the graphical edges that would usually connect nodes to their inputs are hidden here.

Layout heuristics for a given node include:


  • Moving close to and below input nodes.
  • Expanding to fit it's children.
  • Moving away from siblings.
  • Moving towards center of parent.
  • Moving towards origin.

The layout will also try to fill the page intelligently. Try making your browser window wide or tall (or rotating your phone), and it should reorganize itself.

Also, check out the "Fun" node.
